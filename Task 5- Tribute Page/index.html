Albert Einstein

TIMELINE

  • 1879 Albert was born in Ulm, Germany.
  • 1889 Max introduced Albert to math and science. Albert was ten years old.
  • 1894 Albert finished his schooling in Munich with medical training.
  • 1895 Albert moved to Switzerland at 16 years old to attend Aarau Cantonal School.
  • 1896 Albert graduates at the top of his class.
  • 1902 Albert Einstein got a job in the patent office.
  • 1905 Einstein submitted his first paper on his theory of thermodynamics.
  • 1909 Einstein got a job teaching theoretical physics at the University of Zurich.
  • 1911 He was given an invitation to the first-ever world physics conference. He was the youngest physicist there.
  • 1919 After publishing his Theory of Relativity and correctly predicting the effects of a solar eclipse, Einstein's name went around the world.
  • 1922 Einstein was awarded his first Nobel Prize. He was on his way to Japan when he got the news.
  • 1933 He visited America for the first time because he feared the rising Nazi party in Germany.
  • 1945 He made advances in heat, gravity, and relativity, and he published over 300 scientific reports.
  • 1955 He died.
